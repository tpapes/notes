Adduction (name subject to change)

Adduction (name subject to change) is a game I'm making for the May 2024 Buddy Up Jam (Theme: Lost in Space).

Overview

Adduction (name subject to change) is planned to be a 3d space-themed stealth game where you play as an octopus-like alien that is taking revenge on the astronaut crew invading your planet.

Gameplay will involve traversing the area around and inside the astronauts' ship to complete missions that will get you closer to eradicating the crew.

Atmosphere

Adduction (name subject to change) will be horroresque, with all art and atmosphere pointing towards a dark, quiet, empty feeling.

All inspiration for the atmosphere will be posted in the moodboard section.
