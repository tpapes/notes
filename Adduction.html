Adduction (game)

Adduction is a game I'm making for the May 2024 Buddy Up Jam (Theme: Lost in Space).

Overview

Adduction is planned to be a space stealth game where you play as an octopus-like alien that is taking revenge on an astronaut crew invading your planet.

Gameplay will involve traversing the area around and inside the ship to complete missions that will get you closer to eradicating the crew.
